    Khulani Accounting Services

    All-Inclusive Monthly Packages

    Complete Business Financial Management

    Our all-inclusive monthly packages cover everything your business needs to stay financially compliant and efficient. This includes processing monthly transactions, submission of VAT201, EMP201 and UIF, payroll management with payslips, employee reconciliation and IRP5’s, as well as annual financial statements. We also handle company and provisional taxation, CIPC annual returns, director personal income taxation, COIDA submissions, BBBEE letters, and tax clearance certificates. With pre-determined fees, accounting software included, and ongoing monthly analysis and tax planning, we ensure your business runs smoothly and efficiently.

    CIPC Services

    Company Registration & Compliance

    We offer comprehensive CIPC services to help businesses remain compliant and up to date. Our services include company registrations, director and member changes, address updates, share certificates and registers, company restorations, disclosure certificates, NPO registrations, annual returns, and company name changes. We take the complexity out of compliance so you can focus on growth.

    Registrations

    Fast & Efficient Setup

    Our registration services ensure your business is fully set up and compliant from the start. We assist with new company registrations, VAT, PAYE and UIF registration, income and estates tax registrations, workmen’s compensation, e-filing setup, customs registration, and assistance with opening new bank accounts. We make the process simple, fast, and stress-free.

    SARS Services

    Tax Compliance & Submissions

    Our SARS services help you stay compliant with all tax obligations. We handle individual and company taxation, tax clearance certificates, objections and dispute resolution, PAYE submissions, provisional tax submissions, VAT returns, and director taxation. We also assist with updating SARS registered representatives to ensure your records are always accurate and up to date.

    Accounting Services

    Accurate Financial Reporting

    Our accounting services are designed to give you clear and accurate insight into your business finances. We provide monthly bookkeeping, data capture and review, annual financial statements, independent reviews, audits, budgeting and forecasting, cash flow projections, and management accounts. Our goal is to give you the financial clarity needed to make informed business decisions.

    Payroll & HR Services

    Efficient Workforce Management

    We provide reliable payroll and HR services to ensure your workforce is managed efficiently. This includes generating payslips on a monthly, weekly or bi-weekly basis, handling EMP501 reconciliation and IRP5 certificates, managing workmen’s compensation and COIDA, and issuing letters of good standing. Our services help simplify HR processes and ensure compliance with regulations.

    Additional Services

    Business Support Solutions

    We also offer a variety of additional services to support your business operations. These include BEE confirmation certificates, accounting officer letters, Pastel software support, training, employee outsourcing, PAIA manuals, and website design. Our aim is to provide a complete support system that helps your business grow and remain competitive.
